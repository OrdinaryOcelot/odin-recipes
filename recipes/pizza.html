Pizza

Description

This is a great recipe for a no yeast pizza dough for quick but fun evening meal. Kids love it!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Jar of favorite pizza sauce
  • Pizza toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees F
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Combine the water and olive oil in a seprate container
  4. Slowly add the water mixture to the flour, mixing as you go.
  5. Use a spoon (or your hands!) to mix the dough until it comes together in a ball. If it is too sticky, add some more water. If it's too wet, add flour.
  6. Lightly flour a flat surface and work the dough into a round pizza shape
  7. Add in your pizza sauce, and then your favortie ingredients
  8. Place in the center rack of your oven, for 15 minutes.
  9. Slice and enjoy!
